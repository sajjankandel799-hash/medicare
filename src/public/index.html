Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MediCare

How do I book an appointment?
Simply register as a patient, browse available doctors by specialty, and select your preferred time slot. You'll receive instant confirmation.
Is my medical data secure?
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and are fully HIPAA compliant. Your data is stored securely and never shared without your consent.
Can I access my medical records?
Yes! All your medical records are available 24/7 through your patient dashboard. You can view, download, and share them with healthcare providers.
What if I need to cancel an appointment?
You can cancel or reschedule appointments directly from your dashboard. We recommend giving at least 24 hours notice when possible.
Do you offer support for healthcare providers?
Yes! We provide dedicated support for doctors and administrators, including training, technical assistance, and best practices guidance.
